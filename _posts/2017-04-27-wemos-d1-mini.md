  * This is D1 mini, it is a mini WIFI board based on ESP-8266EX.
  * 11 digital input/output pins, all pins have interrupt/pwm/I2C/one-wire
  * supported(except **D0**)
  * 1 analog input(3.3V max input)
  * a Micro USB connection
