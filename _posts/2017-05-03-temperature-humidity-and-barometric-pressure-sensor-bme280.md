  * Supply Voltage: 1.8 &#8211; 5V DC
  * Interface: I2C (up to 3.4MHz), SPI (up to 10 MHz)
  * Operational Range:
  * Temperature: -40 to +85°C
  * Humidity: 0-100%
  * Pressure: 300-1100 hPa
  * Resolution:
  * Temperature: 0.01°C
  * Humidity: 0.008%
  * Pressure: 0.18Pa
  * Accuracy:
  * Temperature: +-1°C
  * Humidity: +-3%
  * Pressure: +-1Pa
  * I2C address
  * SDO LOW ： 0x76
  * SDO HIGH：　0x77
