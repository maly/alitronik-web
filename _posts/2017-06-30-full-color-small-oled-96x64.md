**Feature:**

  * Size: 0.95 inch
  * Resolution: 96*64
  * Drive IC: SSD1331
  * Color: 65536 color
  * Interface: SPI interface
  * PIN NO.: 7Pin
  * Voltage: 3.3V &#8211; 5V

**Pin description:**

  * GND: Power ground
  * VCC: 2.8-5.5V power supply
  * SCL: CLK clock
  * SDA: MOSI data
  * RES: Reset
  * DC: Data / command
  * CS: Chip-select signal
