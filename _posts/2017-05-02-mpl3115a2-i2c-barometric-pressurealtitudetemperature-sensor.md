  * Vin: 3 to 5.5 VDC
  * Logic: 3 to 5V compliant
  * Pressure sensing range: 50-110 kPa (up to 10Km altitude)
  * 1.5 Pa / 0.3 m altitude resolution
  * This board/chip uses I2C 7-bit address 0x60
